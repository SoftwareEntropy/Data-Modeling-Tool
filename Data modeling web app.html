Equipment Inventory Information
To Begin:
Click on any equipment slots to select equipment and their specifications.

Notes:
1) Additional stats such as flames, scrolls, and bonus potential are currently not included in this section due to their wide variances. Instead, include those stats on the 'Additional Stats' section if interested.

2) Certain equipment slots that normally contain class specific items, such as weapons, hats, tops, and bottoms, may display a placeholder image for their respective sets for simplicity. During calculations, the appropriate equipment stats and multipliers for that class will be applied.

3) For classes with multiple weapon choices, such as one-handed sword and shield, or two-handed sword with medallion, the selected sub-weapon option will indicate the setup. Eg. choosing a shield will assume a one-handed weapon. Choosing a setup not applicable for the class will omit sub-weapon stats.

4) Kanna does not have a secondary weapon. Instead, Haku's fan stats will be retrieved from the sub-weapon selection.

5) Heart selection assumes appropriate android is equipped. This means set effects, such as the wondroid +5 attack buff, and pitched boss set effect for black heart is applied.

6) Xenon assumes using thief equipment set
